About Me

I am a senior academic with over 12 years of experience in higher education, health management, and distance learning. Currently serving as a Senior Lecturer and Distance Learning Coordinator at Botho University, Gaborone, Botswana. My expertise includes academic programme design, e-learning integration, and research in digital health education. I have developed and reviewed programmes in collaboration with international universities and industry bodies such as AHIMA and CII.

I’m passionate about using technology to make education accessible and effective. My current focus is on digital transformation in health education and building capacity for AI-enhanced distance learning ecosystems across Southern Africa.
